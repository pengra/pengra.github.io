I'm a big fan of open source things. Here are a few of them available to the public. My projects have helped a wide variety of people, from young coders at hackathons to librarians at various high schools.

Libarians Charities
library.pengra.io is a service that allows highschool librarians to track library usage. Usually it's used to help libraries get more funding by proving students use libraries.
